About Our Prediction Models

Statistical approaches for personalized cancer outcome prediction

Survival Analysis

Our survival prediction models use modified Cox Proportional Hazards approaches integrating clinical factors, biomarkers, and genomic features to create personalized survival curves with confidence intervals.

Recurrence Risk

Recurrence risk prediction leverages pattern recognition across thousands of similar patients to distinguish between local, regional, and distant recurrence probabilities over time.

Treatment Response

Our treatment response models integrate molecular profiles with clinical trial data to predict efficacy and side effect likelihoods for specific treatment regimens in individual patients.

These prediction models are trained on comprehensive datasets from METABRIC, TCGA, and clinical trials. They provide statistical forecasts based on observed outcomes in patients with similar profiles, with validated accuracy and calibration.

Unlike simulation models that explore possible scenarios, prediction models focus on generating the most likely outcomes with statistical confidence based on observed data patterns.
